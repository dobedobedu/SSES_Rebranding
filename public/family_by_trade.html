I. Research Methodology

Research Framework

This report synthesizes findings from three parallel research tracks conducted over a single intensive research cycle. Each track employed the following methodology:

  1. Organization Identification: Systematic identification of major employers, professional organizations, and key institutions within the target geography using industry databases, professional associations, and public records.
  2. Leadership Mapping: Identification of executive leadership, key decision-makers, and influential professionals within each organization using LinkedIn, company websites, and professional directories.
  3. Compensation Analysis: Estimation of compensation ranges using Salary.com, Glassdoor, public payroll records (for public institutions), and industry benchmarking studies.
  4. Geographic Analysis: Mapping of workplace locations relative to SSES campus (zip code 34209) to identify proximity advantages.
  5. Values & Connection Analysis: Assessment of professional values, community involvement patterns, and potential connection points to SSES.
